**Finding houses to flip** is a skill that requires patience and strategic searching. Key strategies include:
  - Exploring various real estate platforms and **real estate agents** for leads.
  - Attending foreclosure auctions where you might purchase a property below market value.

### Step 3: Financial Planning & Budgeting

Once a potential **house to flip** is identified, the next step is financial planning. The breakdown includes:
